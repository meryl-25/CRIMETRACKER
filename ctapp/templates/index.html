Welcome to CrimeTracker

This system enables users to report incidents in real-time, ensuring quick responses from authorities. It includes features such as crime mapping, statistical analysis, and reporting tools to identify high-crime areas and trends. By utilizing a structured database, the system ensures that crime records are easily accessible for investigation and future reference.
